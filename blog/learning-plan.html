Learning Plan

My plan to learn.

22nd April 2021 | Sprint 2

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I want to become familiar with lots of different technologies - various programming languages, cyber security, machine learning, virtual reality, cloud and even quantum computing. I’d like to work on diverse and broad projects that require many different elements and have a big impact on its intended audience. I'm interested in being self employed again at some point but just waiting for the right idea!

Strengths and limitations in relation to learning

My strengths will help me with the learning experience as I am curious and adaptable, seeking out answers to problems. However this can also mean I get distracted so may lose motivation or interest in particular tasks before I complete them. I will be working on my limitations throughout the course.

Develop human skills

Although I have some experience working in teams, I want to strengthen my collaboration and communication skills giving me the ability to articulate myself to a group and solve problems collectively.

Workload commitment

I have cleared about 80% of my schedule for the duration of the course so I can dedicate the recommended time to each day/week. I have weekends available if unforeseen circumstances arise during the week.

I have a good home office setup (dual monitor, powerful pc, comfy office chair) so I can be productive and not get distracted.

Each sprint starts on Sunday where I look over the upcoming sprint activities and plan the work into my week around other commitments I may have. First thing on Monday I review what I completed the previous week and make any changes that I missed. I split my day up into chunks to avoid long periods at my desk.

I find taking regular breaks is a great way to clear the mind and get some perspective if I’m getting overwhelmed with a particular task. Having time away from work makes you more productive at work and I try to use my breaks for something I enjoy doing. Taking time to exercise, spend time with the family and eating delicious food is all beneficial for the mind and soul.

Seeking help commitment

I will always try resolve the problem myself first and maybe have a break and come back to it again. I will be considerate of any deadlines and if I seek out help I’ll do it so that I give people enough time to be able to help me without it impacting their workload too much.

Facilitation team expectation

My expectation is that they will keep me heading in the right direction on my learning journey and identity any major issues that I may not notice myself. I'm terms of technical issues, I expect them to help me find the solution but not actually provide the answer.

Scheduling information

I will need to collect my son from daycare occasionally which will mean I will need to leave bootcamp at 4:30pm. My partner has committed to doing the lions share during this course but she also has work commitments that can't be avoided.

I intend to plan these responsibilities in advance to make sure my workload or peers aren't affected.
